About US

Paraexellence University is a higher learning institution located in Kenya, Africa. It was founded in the year 1906 and as such, the oldest University in Eastern Africa. We offer face to face classes in our three campuses located in Nairobi, Mombasa and Kisumu. We also offer online classes.
